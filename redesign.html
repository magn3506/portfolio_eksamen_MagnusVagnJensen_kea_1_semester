the clients vision
'Green Living Made Easy'

The company is trying to make green-living easier through sustainable and environmentally conscious products.

Analysis

  • Visually, the webpage is very monotonous and not very inviting.

  • The website runs a general user friendly yet dull style throughout the site.

  • The whitespace has been replaced with background image and the golden section is not being used. There is a nice symmetry but a boring dynamic on the side, however, there is too much floating text around the home page.

  • On the home page you can find out relatively quickly which items have content, but at the same time it is confusing because the top section has some of the same function as the footer.

  • The tone is open and welcoming, it must be kept, but the page could benefit from being simplified.

  • The target audience is the usual individual who want to live a healthier lifestyle, but at the same time the sites should also inform and encourage the ignorant to do the same.
